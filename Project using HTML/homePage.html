Taco Trivia

When did taco first appear in India?

Tacos first appeared in India with the introduction of Taco Bell's first outlet in Bangalore in 2010. While some fusion taco restaurants may have existed prior to that, Taco Bell's arrival marked the first major, widely publicized entry of the Mexican-inspired dish into the Indian market.
